# Final Letter

**Voice:** The emotional centre. Slow. Honest. A letter read aloud by someone who means every word.

---

James,

I put off writing this. Not because I had nothing to say. Because I wanted to get it right.

You are capable of more than where you are. I do not say that to cheer you on. I say it because I have watched you.

You do not need to become someone else. You need to become more of yourself.

You will get things wrong. I have. The mistakes that matter are the times you knew better and did nothing.

Stay curious. Stay kind. Answer the people who love you.

Time is the one thing you cannot earn back. Spend it like you mean it.

I built this because I believe in you. The real you. Not the tidy version.

Start.

Love,

Ben

---

**Pause. Then, softly:**

Now close this tab.

Go build something.
